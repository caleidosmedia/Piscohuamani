OUR STORY

Don Ismael de la Quintana made the first bottle of Pisco Huamaní in 1890. His daughter later carried on the tradition. By 1973 her eldest son had grown Pisco Huamaní into one of the most prominent pisco brands in Peru. After the 1970s land expropriations, the family revived the brand and relaunched the long-standing family tradition.

OUR NAME

It was on Hacienda Huamani, in the Valley of Ica, Peru, that our tradition of making pisco began and thus, the reason for the name. In Quechua, the name means mountain spirits who served as guardians fo the Inca people. Andians - descendents of Incans - were our first customers back in 1890 and thus the name resonated.

OUR CRAFT

We make our pisco using a 125 year old recipe created by our founder Don Ismael de la Quintana. It is handcrafted in small batches, adhering to the classic pisco pot distillation method practiced in Peru since the 16th century. Bottled on our farm, Pisco Huamani makes its way from our Hacienda to your glass.

MAKE A DRINK

PISCO SOUR

Ingredients

  •  2 oz Pisco Huamaní
  •  1 oz fresh lime juice
  •  1 oz granulated white sugar
  •  1 oz egg whites (per full blender)
  •  dash of Angostura bitters
  •  Ice

Directions:

Add all ingredients in a blender. Blend on high for 15 seconds, add 5 cubes of ice, and then pulse in the blender 5 times. Strain up into a coupe glass. Garnish with 3 drops of Angostura bitters.

CHILCANO

Ingredients

  •  2 oz Pisco Huamaní
  •  ½ oz fresh lime juice
  •  3 oz de Ginger Ale

Directions:

Build in a tall glass by adding ice cubes, Pisco Huamani and lime juice. Top with ginger ale and gently stir. Garnish with a lime.

CAPITÁN

Ingredients

  •  2 oz Pisco Huamaní
  •  2 oz Vermouth Rosso
  •  1 orange twist (for garnish)

Directions:

Build in a short glass by adding all ingredients and 2 ice cubes. Gently stir and garnish with an orange peel.

PISCO PUNCH

Ingredients

  •  1 bottle of Pisco Huamani
  •  16 oz fresh pinapple juice
  •  6oz simple syrup
  •  ½ fresh pinapple in cubes
  •  7 oz fresh strawberries in cubes
  •  Mint leaves

Directions:

Mix all ingredients in punch bowl or beverage dispenser. Serve in rocks glasses, adding pineapple squares and sliced strawberry. Garnish with mint

PISCO MULE

Ingredients

  •  2 oz Pisco Huamaní
  •  3 oz ginger beer
  •  ½ oz fresh lime juice

Directions:

Build in a rocks glass, filled with ice, garnish with lime wedge.

PALOMETA

Ingredients

  •  1 ½ oz Pisco Huamaní
  •  ½ oz fresh grapefruit juice
  •  ¼ oz fresh lime juice
  •  ½ oz simple syrup

Directions:

Combine all ingredients in a shaker. Fill shaker with ice and shake vigorously. Strain into a tall glass with fresh ice. Garnish with a grapefruit wheel.
